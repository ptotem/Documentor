KYC Checklist
You must know your customer

Identity Proof

  • Passport
  • Driver's License
  • Voter Id Card
  • Photo credit/ Debit card
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Passbook with photograh
  • Letter of Introduction by another Bank

Address Proof

  • Driver's License
  • Ration Card
  • Latest Utility Bills
  • Latest Bank Statement
  • Introduction by an existing account holder

Signature Proof

  • PAN Card
  • Passport
  • Driver's License
  • Bank Attested
  • Voter Id card
